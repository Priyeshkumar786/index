POPULAR DESTINATIONS

CHARDHAM YATRA

The 4 Dham Yatra, also known as Char Dham Yatra, is a pilgrimage in Uttarakhand, India, to four sacred Hindu shrines: Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath. These temples are nestled high in the Himalayas and are considered highly sacred in Hinduism.

12 JYOTIRLINGA YATRA

The 12 Jyotirlingas are a set of 12 revered shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva, considered the most sacred temples in Hinduism. These temples are believed to be where Lord Shiva manifested as a fiery pillar of light, signifying his supreme power and omnipresence.

Ek Dhaam Jagannath Puri, Gangasagar Tirth Yatra

Jagannath Puri is located in Odisha and is dedicated to Lord Jagannath, while Gangasagar is located in West Bengal, where the river Ganges meets the Bay of Bengal. The visit to both these places is also known as "Ek Dham" trip, especially when combined .

Vindhyavasini Devi Darshan, Sitamadhi, Chitrakoot, Naimisharanya Ki Yatra

This journey connects various Shaktipeeths and religious places of India . Vindhyavasini Devi Temple, located in Vindhyachal, is one of the 51 Shaktipeeths. Sitamarhi is a holy place associated with Sita, the wife of Lord Rama.

Nepal Pokhra Kathmandu Manokamna Yatra

a tour package that includes visits to Pokhara, Kathmandu, and the Manokamna Temple in Nepal. These tours often combine scenic beauty, cultural exploration, and religious pilgrimage.

Three Dham And 11 Jyotirling Darshan

A popular pilgrimage tour in India, combining visits to three important Hindu pilgrimage sites (Dham) and the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Flight Tour to Rameshwaram Dham, Pondicherry, Tirupati, Trivandrum, Kanyakumari, Ooty, Mysore

A comprehensive South India pilgrimage and sightseeing experience. Such a tour would typically involve flights between major cities and ground transportation to reach the various destinations, including temples, beaches, and hill stations.

Kamakhya Devi Darshan, Sikkim, Nepal Special Tirth Yatra Tour

These tours typically range from 7 to 15 days and include visits to Kamakhya Temple in Guwahati, the scenic beauty of Sikkim, and potentially Nepal, depending on the specific itinerary. Some tours may also include stops in Darjeeling
